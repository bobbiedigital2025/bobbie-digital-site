<idea>
  **Design Movement**: Neo-Brutalism / Modern Industrial
  **Core Principles**:
  1. **Raw Authenticity**: Unapologetic use of bold borders, high contrast, and structural visibility.
  2. **Functional Clarity**: Information is presented in distinct, card-like containers with clear separation.
  3. **Digital Native**: Embraces the "digital" in Bobbie Digital with monospaced fonts and terminal-inspired elements.
  4. **Action-Oriented**: Buttons and CTAs are prominent, substantial, and demand interaction.

  **Color Philosophy**:
  - **Primary**: Electric Indigo (#4F46E5) - Represents digital energy and innovation.
  - **Secondary**: Stark Black (#111827) & Pure White (#FFFFFF) - For maximum contrast and readability.
  - **Accent**: Neon Pink (#EC4899) - For highlighting key automation triggers and "magic" moments.
  - **Background**: Off-white / Light Gray (#F3F4F6) - To soften the harshness of pure white and add a utilitarian feel.
  *Reasoning*: This palette conveys a sense of robust, powerful software that gets the job done. It's not shy; it's confident and effective.

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  - **Modular Grid**: A strict, visible grid system where content lives in clearly defined boxes.
  - **Asymmetry**: Breaking the grid occasionally with overlapping elements or offset headers to create dynamic tension.
  - **Sidebar Navigation**: A persistent, sticky sidebar for navigation on desktop to mimic a dashboard/app feel, reinforcing the "tool" aspect.

  **Signature Elements**:
  - **Thick Borders**: 2px-3px solid borders on cards and buttons.
  - **Drop Shadows**: Hard, non-blurred drop shadows (box-shadow: 4px 4px 0px #000) to give a tactile, "pop-out" feel.
  - **Tech Motifs**: Subtle circuit board patterns or dot grids in backgrounds.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  - **Tactile Feedback**: Buttons depress visually (translate) on click.
  - **Hover States**: Sharp color inversions or border color changes on hover.
  - **Micro-interactions**: Loading states that look like terminal command executions.

  **Animation**:
  - **Snappy Transitions**: Instant or very fast ease-out transitions (no slow fades).
  - **Staggered Entrance**: Elements slide in from the bottom or side with a slight bounce.

  **Typography System**:
  - **Headings**: **Space Grotesk** or **Chakra Petch** - Technical, futuristic, yet readable.
  - **Body**: **Inter** or **DM Sans** - Clean, neutral, highly legible for density.
  - **Code/Accents**: **JetBrains Mono** or **Fira Code** - For "automation" snippets and technical terms.
</idea>
</text>

  **Design Movement**: Glassmorphism / Ethereal Tech
  **Core Principles**:
  1. **Transparency & Depth**: Using blur effects and semi-transparent layers to create a sense of hierarchy and modern sophistication.
  2. **Fluidity**: Organic shapes and smooth gradients that suggest seamless automation and flow.
  3. **Light & Glow**: Using light sources to guide the eye, representing the "AI" and "intelligence" aspect.
  4. **Minimalist Luxury**: Clean lines, ample whitespace, and a premium feel that elevates the brand perception.

  **Color Philosophy**:
  - **Primary**: Deep Violet (#7C3AED) to Soft Blue (#60A5FA) Gradient - Represents the seamless transition of workflows.
  - **Secondary**: Glassy White (rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.1)) - For card backgrounds.
  - **Accent**: Cyan / Teal (#2DD4BF) - For success states and "active" indicators.
  - **Background**: Deep Navy / Dark Mode Base (#0F172A) - To make the glass effects and glows pop.
  *Reasoning*: This palette feels futuristic, high-tech, and "magical," aligning with the promise of AI and effortless automation.

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  - **Floating Elements**: Content cards that appear to float above the background.
  - **Z-Axis Layering**: Using depth to separate background (abstract shapes), mid-ground (content), and foreground (navigation/modals).
  - **Centralized Focus**: Key messages centered with radiating elements.

  **Signature Elements**:
  - **Frosted Glass**: Backdrop-filter: blur() on cards and nav bars.
  - **Soft Glows**: Box-shadows with color to simulate light emission.
  - **Mesh Gradients**: Subtle, moving background gradients that add life without distraction.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  - **Smooth Flow**: Hover effects that gently lift elements or increase their glow.
  - **Parallax**: Subtle movement of background elements relative to scroll.

  **Animation**:
  - **Slow & Smooth**: Long duration ease-in-out transitions.
  - **Fade & Scale**: Elements gently fade in and scale up.

  **Typography System**:
  - **Headings**: **Outfit** or **Plus Jakarta Sans** - Geometric, modern, friendly.
  - **Body**: **Satoshi** or **General Sans** - Clean, modern sans-serifs with character.

<idea>
  **Design Movement**: Swiss Style / International Typographic Style (Digital Adaptation)
  **Core Principles**:
  1. **Objective Clarity**: Prioritizing the message above all else with strict hierarchy.
  2. **Grid Systems**: Mathematical precision in layout.
  3. **Asymmetric Balance**: Creating visual interest through weight and placement rather than decoration.
  4. **Typography as Image**: Using large, bold type as the primary visual element.

  **Color Philosophy**:
  - **Primary**: International Orange (#FF4500) or Swiss Red (#D80000) - Bold, attention-grabbing, classic.
  - **Secondary**: Neutral Greys (#E5E5E5 to #333333) - To provide a structured backdrop.
  - **Accent**: Cobalt Blue (#0047AB) - For links and interactive elements.
  - **Background**: White (#FFFFFF) - The canvas for the content.
  *Reasoning*: This style communicates reliability, precision, and professionalism. It says "we are serious about business automation."

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  - **Strict Grid**: Visible or invisible 12-column grid.
  - **Negative Space**: Large areas of white space to frame content.
  - **Horizontal Rhythm**: Strong horizontal lines separating sections.

  **Signature Elements**:
  - **Huge Typography**: Headlines that span the full width or take up significant vertical space.
  - **Rule Lines**: Thin horizontal and vertical lines to define structure.
  - **Photography**: High-contrast, black and white photography with color overlays.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  - **Direct**: Instant feedback, no unnecessary flourishes.
  - **Cursor Effects**: Custom cursor that changes based on context (e.g., "View" or "Read").

  **Animation**:
  - **Typography Reveals**: Text sliding up from masks.
  - **Line Drawing**: Borders drawing themselves upon scroll.

  **Typography System**:
  - **Headings**: **Helvetica Now** (or **Inter Tight** as free alternative) - The classic, refined.
  - **Body**: **Inter** or **Roboto** - Neutral, readable.
</idea>
